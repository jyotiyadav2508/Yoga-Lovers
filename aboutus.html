Our Yoga Programs

Yoga For Golden-Agers


Retirement – the new beginning. Time to get in shape both physically and mentally so you can enjoy your retirement to the maximum.

Our senior years means we have to work harder to keep us mentally sharp and physically fit.

YOGA-LOVERS will help you do that. Our courses are available in your home and tailored just for you no matter what shape you are in. So you don’t have to be physically fit or ‘flexible’!

All our courses are available for golden-agers.

So contact us - its never too late to start your own yoga journey and seek to transform the quality of your life.

Child Yoga


Yoga and mindfulness offer psychological benefits for children as well.

A growing body of research has already shown that yoga can improve focus, memory, self-esteem, academic performance, and classroom behavior, and can even reduce anxiety and stress in children.

Let's start improving balance, strength, endurance, and aerobic capacity in children.

Meditation


Do you have stress, anxiety, low concentration on work, unbalenced emotions, then start Meditation.

Meditation can give you a sense of calm, peace and balance that can benefit both your emotional well-being and your overall health.Meditation cultivates self-awareness, and provides the optimum conditions for practicing the skill of mindfulness.

Yoga for Beginners


Are you still at the very beginning of your yoga journey ?

Our Yoga for Beginners guide will give you the best tips, guidelines, advice & recommendations you need to start a successful yoga practice.

Advanced Yoga


Do you want to take your yoga practice to next level?

Advanced yoga enables you to perform complex yoga poses with higher concentration for longer duration.

It brings your physical fitness and your mental well-being to the next level.
